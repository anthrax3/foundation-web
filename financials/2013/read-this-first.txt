This directory contains OpenBSD Foundation financials for FY 2013. 

Supporting Documents include: 

"paypal2013.txt" is a version of the paypal csv/pdf account documents
which which were obtained directly from paypal. We can not make the
original paypal documents public as they contain private identifying
details of anonymous donors.

"CIBC-2013-X.jpg" are images of the full year bank statments for the
foundation's CIBC bank account. They has been manually redacted to
remove donor information. We can not make the original statements
public as they contain private identifying details of anonymous
donors.
